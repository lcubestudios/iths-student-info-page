Judging Criteria
  • 01
    Impact & Relevance Does this solve a real problem at ITHS? Would people actually use it?
  • 02
    Technical Execution Is the code working? Is the architecture sensible? Does the demo run?
  • 03
    Design & Usability Is the interface clear and intuitive? Does it respect the user?
  • 04
    Pitch & Presentation Is the problem clearly framed? Is the solution well communicated?
  • 05
    Creativity & Ambition Does the project surprise us? Did the team stretch beyond the obvious?
